Book at Shinbashi Shimidu

Thank you for visiting Shinbashi Shimizu's online reservation page.

We look forward to receiving your reservation.

Reservations can be made daily at 12:00 noon, up to 7 days in advance.

■Regarding International Guests
We only accept reservations when accompanied by someone who understands Japanese.
If a guest does not understand Japanese, the reservation will be canceled on the day and a 100% cancellation policy will apply.

■Important Notes
*If anyone other than the person with the reservation shows up, they will be denied entry and will be treated as a no-show.
*You may be asked to present identification upon entry. If you do not have identification, we may ask a substitute to come in your place.
*Please refrain from wearing strong scents such as perfume or cologne. Please be sure to inform any accompanying persons.
*Taking photos or videos inside the store is prohibited.
*Please make sure that the person who made the reservation comes in person. We do not allow substitutes or transfers of reservations to prevent any issues.
*Due to limited space, we are unable to accommodate wheelchair users. *18 years of age and older (only those who can dine on the same course as adults)
*Reservations are limited to 1-3 people per group.

Telephone inquiries: 03-3591-5763

Requests

Extensive allergies or ingredients you cannot eat that would affect two or more dishes within the course will be treated as a cancellation; depending on when you notify us, the cancellation policy may apply. Please check this before making your reservation.
